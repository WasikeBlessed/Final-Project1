Master the Smart Crop System: Your Ultimate Guide

Getting Started

  1. Access the Predict Tool: Click on Predict from the top menu to begin.
  2. Enter Data: Input accurate soil and climate details (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium, Temperature, Humidity, pH, Rainfall) based on your farm's conditions.
  3. Submit Prediction: Hit the Predict Crop button to receive your recommendation.
  4. Review Results: Explore the recommended crop, its requirements, and detailed insights on the results page.

Expert Tips for Success

  • Accurate Data is Key: Use soil test kits or local weather data for precise inputs to get the best recommendations.
  • Seasonal Timing: Plant crops according to the recommended season for optimal yield (e.g., Rice in monsoon, Wheat in winter).
  • Soil Preparation: Prepare your soil based on the crop's pH and nutrient needs before planting.
  • Monitor Growth: Regularly check crop health and adjust irrigation or fertilizers as suggested in the guide.

Crop Management Essentials

Once you receive your crop recommendation, follow these steps to maximize your harvest:

  • Irrigation: Ensure water levels match the crop's needs (e.g., flooding for Rice, moderate for Wheat).
  • Fertilization: Apply fertilizers based on the predicted nutrient requirements to avoid over- or under-feeding.
  • Pest Control: Use organic or chemical methods to protect your crops from common pests and diseases.
  • Harvesting: Harvest at the right time to ensure quality and quantity, as guided by local agricultural calendars.

Visualizing Your Farm

Imagine a lush field with your recommended crop thriving! Picture rows of green Rice paddies reflecting the sun or golden Wheat swaying in the breeze. Use the prediction data to plan your field layout, ensuring proper spacing and sunlight exposure for the best growth.
